WeatherTec Academy for Water and Climate Security

Customized Training for Professionals

The WeatherTec Academy makes available the expertise of the WeatherTec Group of scientist. The Academy has proven excellence professional training programs:

Advanced Executive Training Courses for local meteorologists and climatologists. Additional training in numerical weather prediction for IT experts taught by German professors.

Know-how exchange with high-tech companies

The Academy for Water and Climate Security provides "learning by doing" practical courses using the most advanced equipment in meteorology, climatology, and related sciences. It focuses on learning how to directly use the sophisticated equipment in real situations such as the use cases shown on this website. The equipment instrumentation is the latest state of the art advances in weather monitoring and intervention. The applications were selected for quality, practical use, robustness, and simple maintenance. These are critical attributes for creating a sustainable technical foundation to provide the best meteorological services that meets the highest global standards of excellence, and our deployments are the best in the world for countries with semi – or arid climates.
